# Firesand Ltd

## Bulletproof Cybersecurity & Compliance Solutions

Firesand is a specialist cybersecurity consultancy based in Milton Keynes, UK, delivering tailored security solutions. We specialize in offensive security testing and comprehensive compliance solutions for organizations worldwide. With over 9 years of proven expertise, we serve organisations across highly regulated sectors including iGaming, banking, insurance, education, and maritime.

## Our Expertise

We are a team of security advisers, architects, consultants, and engineers holding extensive industry certifications including SABSA, CCISO, CISSP, and CIPP/E. Our approach combines deep technical knowledge with business acumen to build strategic security roadmaps aligned with your objectives.

### Our Services

**Security Strategy & Architecture**
Evolve your security posture in line with business goals, including cloud and hybrid migrations, enterprise security architecture, and ongoing consultancy.

**Penetration Testing & Vulnerability Management**
Identify security weaknesses through comprehensive testing, vulnerability audits, and Cyber Essentials assessments to pre-empt threats before they materialise.

**Data Privacy**
GDPR compliance services, training, and outsourced Data Protection Officer (DPO) support. Industry-accredited compliance including ISO 27001 and PCI DSS consultancy.

**Security Training**
Bespoke training programs covering general cyber and information security, plus Firesand Certified Secure Software Engineering Training to embed secure practices across your organisation.

## Our Approach

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Every engagement is tailored to your specific processes, requirements, and risk profile. We're vendor-agnostic, working with your existing security investments to maximise value whilst delivering cutting-edge protection.

Security and compliance aren't IT tasks - they're business risks. Our holistic methodology encompasses people, processes, and technology to ensure secure behaviours permeate every level of your organisation.
